WORKFLOW 2: INTERACTIVE COOKING & RECIPE CREATION
---------------------------------------------------
User Journey: "I want to cook this recipe and maybe create my own"

FEATURES
1. Interactive Cooking Mode

-Step-by-step interface

-Progress bar

-Timers for each step

-Next/previous buttons

-Mark as completed

2. Post-Cooking Actions

-Rate recipe (1-5 stars)

-Write review

-Save to favorites

-Mark ingredients as used (updates pantry)

3. Create Your Own Recipe

-Add recipe title/description

-Add ingredients with quantities

-Add step-by-step instructions

-Set difficulty/time

-Add dietary tags

-Publish or save as draft

4. View Other Users' Recipes

-Browse community recipes

-Filter by creator

-See ratings/reviews

-Save to your collection
